A little bit about me.

Way back when dinosaurs like the Commodore 64 first ruled the Earth, in the years before BluRay, before DVD's, before CD's, before disks of any kind, I spent hours typing out programs from magazines so my younger brother and sister could play them. Sometimes there were bugs in the code, sometimes I typed it wrong but I always found the error and fixed it... and as long as no one turned off the computer, we were set! Thankfully it wasn't long until we got a tape player and at least could save the programs for later!

Since then I've played around with different languages. I've coded macros in WordBasic, (not VisualBasic, WordBasic) and loved the challenge of making things work the way I wanted them to. I've created Excel spreadsheets to look up and display information based on a product code. I wanted to build a more sophisticated interface, but didn't have the tools for the job. This peaked my curiousity and I couldn't seem to let it go. The world of web development is just opening for me, but the possibilities are endless.
